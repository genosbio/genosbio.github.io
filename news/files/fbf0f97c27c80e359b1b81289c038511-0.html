Ozcorp Scientific Unveils GENOS to Propel Biotechnology Research Through Tailored Bioinformatics Services

Dec/05/2023 – Press
SAN JUAN, Puerto Rico – December 11, 2023 – Ozcorp Scientific, a biotech venture studio, proudly announces the official emergence of GENOS, marking its decisive step out of stealth-mode and into the realm of pioneering bioinformatics solutions. Spearheaded by Dr. Osvaldo D. Rivera, former Bioinformatics Scientist, GENOS stands poised to revolutionize the landscape of bioinformatics services, positioned strategically within Puerto Rico, a recently designated Tech Hub to spur american innovation in biotechnologies, biopharmaceuticals and medical device manufacturing.

After an extensive training in bioinformatics throughout PhD programs and industry, Dr. Rivera has meticulously crafted GENOS to bridge the gap in data analysis, harmonization, visualization, and interpretation for investigators. Located in Puerto Rico, a nucleus for cutting-edge research and innovation, GENOS envisions transformative scientific collaborations that drive groundbreaking discoveries.

GENOS operates as a trailblazing bioinformatics-as-a-service company, committed to supporting investigators by shouldering the intricacies of data analysis. Our mission is clear: to become the premier service provider of bioinformatics by leveraging industry-standard tools and practices. Irrespective of team size, GENOS empowers biotech innovators to transcend the confines of their research, embodying our ethos of 'Bioinformatics for Everyone.'

In the upcoming year, GENOS is fervently focused on amplifying its local clientele base, forging alliances through collaborative research grants, and engineering bespoke software products designed to democratize access to bioinformatics capabilities.

Our commitment to fostering innovation and collaboration within Puerto Rico's burgeoning scientific community is unwavering.


- Osvaldo D. Rivera, PhD
Founder & Head of Research
GENOS LLC


"This unveiling marks a pivotal moment for GENOS as we step into the limelight, extending our services and expertise to revolutionize the bioinformatics landscape," expressed Dr. Rivera, Founder and CEO of GENOS. "Our commitment to fostering innovation and collaboration within Puerto Rico's burgeoning scientific community is unwavering."
