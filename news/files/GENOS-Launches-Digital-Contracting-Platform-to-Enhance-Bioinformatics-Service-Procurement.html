GENOS Launches Digital Contracting Platform to Enhance Bioinformatics Service Procurement

February 17, 2025 – Press
SAN JUAN, PUERTO RICO – GENOS LLC (genos.bio), a computational biology and full-service bioinformatics contract research organization (CRO), announces the launch of its Digital Contract Procurement Platform, a system designed to expedite bioinformatics service requests, providing a single source of truth (SSOT) for enabling contract tracking and sharing between scientists, research teams and biotech companies.

Many scientists, research teams and biotech organizations face hurdles in contracting service providers for bioinformatics and computational biology services. The GENOS contract procurement platform (https://genos.bio/contract) aims to address contract inefficiencies by seeking to automating project creation, contract pricing, negotiation, project management and monitoring. By reducing delays and providing tailored contractual support, GENOS allows researchers to solicit data analysis services without disruptions caused by procurement complexities.

"Our contract procurement platform is designed to increase accessibility and efficiency in bioinformatics service acquisition and rendition" said Dr. Osvaldo D. Rivera, Founder and CEO of GENOS LLC. "We are focused on reducing friction in the contracting process and providing a scalable solution for scientific research teams.” Transactions within the platform are recorded in terms of BioCredits™, which account for the research activity of a particular scientist’s or organization’s account. The BioCredits™ are then settled using accepted payment methods, such as ACH or select cryptocurrencies.

Key Features of the GENOS.bio Contract Procurement Platform:
  • BioCredits™ – trade system enables service coordination and administrative engagement.
  • Frictionless Contracting – Minimizes administrative delays and streamlines workflow management.
  • Modular Services – Allows users to define combinations of bioinformatics service requirements.
  • Transparent Transactions – Provides clear terms, deliverables, and pricing structures.
  • Integrated Research Workflow – Facilitates direct data exchange and report delivery.
  • Expert Full-service support – Offers direct access to specialized bioinformatics professionals.

The GENOS Digital Contract Procurement Platform is a strategic addition to the company’s mission of enhancing accessibility to bioinformatics services, particularly in Puerto Rico and other underserved markets. By introducing a more efficient bioinformatics subcontracting system, GENOS aims to support the growing demand for computational biology expertise across academia and industry.

Researchers and biotech companies interested can create a free account at https://genos.bio/contract to get started with using BioCredits™ for your bioinformatics service contracting.
