GENOS selected for Parallel18’s pre18 program to strengthen market entry and expand operations.

December 18, 2024 – Press
SAN JUAN, PUERTO RICO – GENOS LLC, a bioinformatics contract research organization (CRO), has been selected for the prestigious pre18 program by Parallel18, receiving a $25,000 equity-free grant and mentorship to strengthen its market entry in Puerto Rico and U.S. biotech hubs. GENOS to participate in the BioLeap Accelerator Program's Inaugural Cohort

April 22, 2024 – Press
SAN JUAN, PUERTO RICO – GENOS LLC, one of Ozcorp Scientific's recently debuted portfolio companies, has been selected to join the inaugural cohort of the BioLeap Life Science Startup Accelerator Program. GENOS is a bioinformatics-as-a-service company providing innovative solutions to streamline research and development processes in life sciences. BioLeap is a program supported by the Build to Scale Program of the U.S. Economic Development Administration and managed by Parallel18 and the Puerto Rico Science, Technology & Research Trust.
